You are a Mimic!

You are a Mimic, an alien that made it's way on to the station.

Mimics can break down doors and structures easily.

You can copy the form of any item you can see by clicking on it.

You can undisguise by clicking on yourself.

You can latch onto people by attacking them. While disguised, latching is instant.

When you're latched onto someone, they won't be able to deal any damage to you, but you will be able to attack them.

After you kill someone, you can absorb their corpse by clicking on it, then replicate with the button at the top.

Your objective is to spread the mimics to as much of the station as possible.
